**2. Understanding of Risks**
I acknowledge that all medical and aesthetic procedures carry inherent risks, including but not limited to:
- Bruising and swelling
- Pain and discomfort
- Infection
- Allergic reactions
- Unsatisfactory results
- Scarring

- Rare but serious complications

**3. Informed Consent**
I have been provided information about:
- The procedure(s) to be performed
- Expected outcomes
- Potential risks and complications
- Alternative treatments
- What to expect before, during, and after treatment

**4. Truthful Information**
I have provided accurate and complete information about:
- My medical history
- Current medications
- Allergies

- Current health conditions

**5. Following Instructions**
I agree to follow all pre-treatment and post-treatment instructions provided to me. I understand that failure to do so may affect my results and increase risks.

To the fullest extent permitted by law, I release, waive, and discharge Hello Gorgeous Med Spa, its owners, employees, and contractors from any and all claims, liabilities, or damages arising from my treatment, except in cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct.

**7. Assumption of Risk**
I assume full responsibility for any risks associated with the procedures I receive, including those risks that are unknown or unanticipated.

**8. No Guarantee**
I understand that no guarantees have been made regarding the outcome of my treatment. Results vary by individual.

## Financial Agreement

### Payment Terms and Conditions

**1. Payment Due**
Payment is due at time of service unless other arrangements have been made.

**2. Payment Methods**
We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, CareCredit, Cherry, and Afterpay.

**3. Deposits**
- Deposits may be required for certain services
- Deposits are non-refundable if cancelled within 24 hours

**4. Cancellation Policy**
- 24+ hours notice: No charge
- Less than 24 hours: Up to 50% of service fee
- No-show: Up to 100% of service fee

**5. Package Services**
- Packages are non-transferable
- Packages are non-refundable after first use
- Packages expire per terms at purchase

Accounts past due may be sent to collections. Patient is responsible for collection costs and attorney fees.
