## BINDING ARBITRATION AGREEMENT

**PLEASE READ THIS AGREEMENT CAREFULLY. IT AFFECTS YOUR LEGAL RIGHTS.**

---

## 1. Agreement to Arbitrate

By signing this agreement, you agree that any dispute, claim, or controversy arising out of or relating to your treatment at Hello Gorgeous Med Spa shall be resolved exclusively through **binding arbitration** rather than in a court of law.

---

## 2. Claims Covered

This agreement applies to all claims, including but not limited to:

- Medical malpractice
- Professional negligence
- Failure to obtain informed consent
- Breach of contract
- Personal injury
- Product liability
- Breach of fiduciary duty
- Any other claim related to services provided

---

## 3. Claims NOT Covered

This agreement does NOT apply to:
- Small claims court actions (under $10,000)
- Collection of undisputed debts
- Injunctive relief for intellectual property

---

## 4. Waiver of Rights

By agreeing to arbitration, you are **waiving the following rights**:

### Right to Jury Trial
You will not have a jury decide your case. An arbitrator will make all decisions.

### Right to Court Proceeding
Your dispute will not be heard in state or federal court.

### Right to Class Action
You cannot participate in or be represented in a class action lawsuit.

### Right to Appeal
Arbitration decisions are final and binding with very limited appeal rights.

---

## 5. Arbitration Process

### Administrator
American Arbitration Association (AAA)

### Rules
AAA Healthcare Arbitration Rules

### Location
DuPage County, Illinois

### Selection of Arbitrator
- Neutral arbitrator selected from AAA panel
- Must have healthcare industry experience
- Both parties may strike candidates

### Discovery
- Limited discovery as determined by arbitrator
- Exchange of documents
- Depositions may be permitted

### Hearing
- In-person or virtual (by agreement)
- Formal rules of evidence relaxed
- Both parties may present witnesses

### Decision
- Written decision within 30 days of hearing
- Decision is final and binding
- Judgment may be entered in any court

---

## 6. Costs

### Filing Fees
- Shared equally or as determined by arbitrator
- AAA fees per AAA schedule

### Attorney Fees
- Each party responsible for their own
- Arbitrator may award fees to prevailing party

### Arbitrator Fees
- Shared equally unless otherwise determined

---

## 7. Confidentiality

All aspects of arbitration are confidential:
- Proceedings
- Documents
- Testimony
- Decision

Neither party may disclose except as required by law.

---

## 8. Statute of Limitations

Claims must be filed within:
- 2 years for medical malpractice (Illinois law)
- Applicable statute of limitations for other claims

---

## 9. Severability

If any part of this agreement is unenforceable, the remaining parts remain in effect.

---

## 10. Right to Opt Out

You have the right to **opt out** of this arbitration agreement:

### To Opt Out:
1. Send written notice within **30 days** of first treatment
2. Include your name, address, and statement that you opt out
3. Mail to:
   
   Hello Gorgeous Med Spa  
   ATTN: Arbitration Opt-Out  
   74 W. Washington St  
   Oswego, IL 60543

If you do not opt out within 30 days, you are bound by this agreement.

---

## 11. Right to Consult Attorney

You have the right to:
- Consult an attorney before signing
- Take this agreement home to review
- Ask questions before agreeing
